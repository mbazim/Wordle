Welcome to Wordle

How to Play

Guess the hidden 5-letter word in 6 tries. After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

Tile Colors Explained:

Correct Letter, Correct Position
Correct Letter, Wrong Position
Letter Not in Word

Game Rules

  • You have 6 attempts to guess the word
  • Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word
  • After each guess, you'll get feedback on your letters
  • Green means the letter is correct and in the right spot
  • Yellow means the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot
  • Gray means the letter is not in the word
